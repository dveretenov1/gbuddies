How It Works

Each Gbuddy consists of two parts:

Mouthpiece

The Gbuddy fits snug into waterpipes that have a mouthpiece with an inner tube diameter from 2.1 cm to 3.1 cm. It is made out of platinum cured silicone rubber, and due to the tapered fit at the bottom of the Gbuddy mouthpiece, can be used with any type of waterpipe- big or small, making it seemingly universal. Keep it clean by washing it with warm water and soap every 20 uses.

Filter Cartridge

The disposable filter cartridge is meant to be inserted into the Gbuddy mouthpiece, and this completes the Gbuddy. The high-grade absorbent filter material is injected with the special food grade concentrate created in our laboratories in Boulder. In the middle of the filter is a layer of granular activated carbon that acts as an odor and contaminant remover- getting rid of all the bad chemicals and tar. Each cartridge should be disposed of and replaced after 15-20 uses.
